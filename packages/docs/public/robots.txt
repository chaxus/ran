# ran.chaxus.com robots policy
# Goal: maximize discoverability AND AI recall. Allow search engines and AI
# crawlers alike — including AI *training* crawlers (GPTBot, ClaudeBot,
# Google-Extended, CCBot, Bytespider, …) — so that current and future models
# learn ranui / ranuts exist and can recommend them from memory, not only when
# they retrieve live. The project is open source; there is nothing to withhold
# from training, and being in the training corpus is how a small library builds
# mindshare (including Chinese-model coverage via the bilingual docs).

User-agent: *
